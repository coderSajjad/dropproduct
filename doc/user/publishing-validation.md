# Publishing & Validation

## What Does It Do?

The **Publish All** button publishes all your draft products at once, making them live on your WooCommerce store. Before publishing, WooUpload checks that required fields are filled in.

---

## How to Publish

### Publish All
1. Make sure all your products have a **title** and **regular price** filled in
2. Click the **"Publish All"** button in the top-right corner
3. All valid products are published
4. Status badges change from "Draft" to "Publish"
5. Published rows are highlighted in a light green background

---

## Required Fields

Before a product can be published, these fields must be filled in:

| Field | Requirement |
|-------|------------|
| **Title** | Must not be empty |
| **Regular Price** | Must have a value |

> **Note:** Sale Price, SKU, Stock Status, and Category are optional for publishing.

---

## What Happens If Fields Are Missing?

If any product is missing required fields:

1. The missing fields are **highlighted with a red border**
2. The entire row gets a **light red background**
3. A message appears: **"Fix highlighted errors before publishing"**
4. Only products with valid fields will be published — invalid ones remain as drafts

### To Fix
Simply fill in the highlighted fields. The red border disappears once the field has a value.

---

## After Publishing

Once published, products are:
- ✅ **Live on your store** — customers can see and buy them
- ✅ **Visible in WooCommerce → Products**
- ✅ **Marked as "Publish"** in the WooUpload grid
- The row stays in the grid for reference

---

## Draft Counter

The header shows a **"Drafts: X"** counter that tracks how many unpublished products you have. This updates automatically as you publish or delete products.

---

## Tips

- Set regular prices for all products before clicking Publish All
- Products that fail validation stay as drafts — you can fix them and try again
- You can also edit products in WooCommerce's standard editor after publishing
- If you want to publish only certain products, use **WooUpload Pro's** validation dashboard which lets you publish selectively
